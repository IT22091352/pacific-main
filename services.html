Our Services

What We Offer

Comprehensive Tour Services

At Wander Lanka Tours, we offer comprehensive travel services tailored to your needs. From airport transfers to guided tours and event management, we ensure your Sri Lankan adventure is unforgettable, safe, and perfectly tailored to your needs. We provide both male and female professional drivers to ensure your comfort and preferences are met.

Guided Cultural Tours

Immerse yourself in Sri Lanka's rich cultural heritage with our expert-guided tours. Visit ancient temples, UNESCO World Heritage Sites, and experience traditional ceremonies and festivals.

  • Ancient city tours (Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa)
  • Temple visits with cultural insights
  • Traditional dance performances
  • Local village experiences
  • Expert historical commentary

Experience Sri Lanka with Wander Lanka Tours

Experience the thrill of encountering Sri Lanka's incredible wildlife in their natural habitats. Our safari tours offer the best chances to see elephants, leopards, sloth bears, and exotic birds.

  • Yala National Park safaris
  • Udawalawe elephant watching
  • Bird watching tours
  • Marine wildlife excursions
  • Professional naturalist guides

Beach & Coastal Experiences

Relax on pristine beaches, enjoy water sports, and explore charming coastal towns. From surfing to whale watching, we offer the complete beach experience.

  • Beach resort recommendations
  • Water sports activities
  • Whale & dolphin watching
  • Snorkeling & diving trips
  • Coastal town tours

Adventure & Trekking

For the adventurous spirit, we offer exciting trekking experiences through mountains, forests, and tea plantations. Challenge yourself while enjoying breathtaking views.

  • Adam's Peak pilgrimage trek
  • Knuckles Mountain Range hiking
  • Ella Rock & Little Adam's Peak
  • White water rafting
  • Rock climbing experiences

Tea Plantation Tours

Discover the world-famous Ceylon tea in the misty hill country. Visit working tea estates, learn about tea production, and enjoy scenic train rides through tea plantations.

  • Tea factory visits
  • Tea tasting sessions
  • Scenic train journeys
  • Hill country exploration
  • Colonial bungalow stays

Customized Private Tours

Create your perfect itinerary with our fully customizable private tours. We work with you to design a journey that matches your interests, schedule, and budget.

  • Personalized itinerary planning
  • Private vehicle & professional driver
  • Male or female driver options available
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Accommodation booking
  • 24/7 support during tour

Airport Transfer Services

Start your Sri Lankan adventure stress-free with our reliable airport transfer services. We provide comfortable, safe, and punctual transportation from Bandaranaike International Airport to any destination across Sri Lanka.

  • 24/7 airport pickup & drop-off
  • Flight tracking for delays
  • Meet & greet service
  • Air-conditioned vehicles
  • Professional male or female drivers
  • Luggage assistance

Taxi Services

Explore Sri Lanka at your own pace with our flexible taxi services. Whether you need transportation for a day, a week, or longer, we provide comfortable vehicles with experienced drivers who know the island inside out.

  • Daily, weekly & monthly rentals
  • Point-to-point transfers
  • City tours & sightseeing
  • English-speaking drivers
  • Choice of male or female drivers
  • Competitive rates
  • Clean & well-maintained vehicles
